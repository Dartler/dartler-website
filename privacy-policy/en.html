Privacy Policy for Dartler Standalone

This Privacy Policy describes how the Dartler Standalone app (the "App") handles information.

1. Name and Contact Information of the Controller

The controller within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other national data protection laws of the member states as well as other data protection regulations is:

Dartler Suite

Email: dartler.suite@gmail.com

2. What Data Does the App Collect?

The App exclusively collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Player Information: Names of players you create.
  • Game Statistics: Data related to games you've played, associated with the created players.

3. How Is This Data Stored and Used?

All the data mentioned above is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. It is used to:

  • Manage your player profiles.
  • Display your game statistics within the App.
  • Allow you to import statistics from other apps and integrate them into your local database.

Important: This data is not transmitted to us or third parties, unless you use the optional Google Drive backup feature.

4. Control Over Your Data

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • You can view and delete players and their statistics within the App at any time.
  • You can delete imported statistics at any time.

5. Optional Google Drive Backup Feature

The App offers an optional feature to back up your game statistics to your personal Google Drive account.

  • If you use this feature:
    • The App will ask for your consent to sign in with your Google account.
    • It will use the Google Drive API to create a cloud backup of your local statistics.
    • This backup will be stored exclusively in the dedicated "App Data folder" of your personal Google Drive account, which only this App can access. It's not directly visible to other apps or users.
    • You can use this backup at any time to restore your statistics in the App.
  • What data is backed up? Only the player information and game statistics mentioned in Section 1 are backed up.
  • Data Privacy with Google Drive: The use of Google Drive is subject to Google's privacy policy. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information.
  • Your Choice: Using this feature is completely optional. If you choose not to use it, all your data will remain stored exclusively on your device.

6. Firebase APIs, Cloud Functions, and App Check

The App uses Firebase services, including Cloud Functions and App Check, to enhance functionality and security:

  • Firebase Cloud Functions: These are used to securely process certain app-related tasks in the cloud. No personal data is transmitted unless explicitly required for the feature you use.
  • Firebase App Check: This feature helps protect the App and its backend services from abuse by verifying that requests come from the legitimate App. App Check may collect device-related information to perform this verification.

Logging Function Access for Rate Limiting:

To ensure stability, availability, and protection against abuse of our Cloud Functions, we log the time of the last access. For this, we use the Firebase App ID (a pseudonymous identifier of your app installation, not directly linked to personal data). This data is used solely for rate limiting to control the number of function calls and ensure fair usage and system stability. Processing is based on our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR. No manual linking with individual user data occurs. Access times are stored only for the short, dynamic period necessary for effective rate limiting (e.g., minutes to hours), unless required for abuse tracking or by legal obligations.

Data Privacy with Firebase: The use of Firebase services is subject to Google's privacy policy. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will inform you of any significant changes by publishing the new Privacy Policy within the App and on the Google Play Store. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at dartler.suite@gmail.com.
